He's a licensed attorney, an 8-time published author, a combat veteran, a motivational speaker, a computer and web programmer, a feature filmmaker, an actor, a screenwriter, and the founder and executive director of a worldwide not-for-profit. About the Program

The program is broken up into 2 parts. Part 1 takes place in January, is very intensive, and involves creating a plan unlike any you've ever used. Part 2 takes place every three months thereafter, throughout the year, keeping you on track and helping you to update the plan as things change.

If you choose to pay for both up front, it's a flat fee of $1250--a savings of $50.
